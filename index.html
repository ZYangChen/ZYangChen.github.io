About

Short Biography

Ziyang Chen​ is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology at the Media Analytics & Computing (MAC) Laboratory, Xiamen University, under the supervision of Prof. Liujuan Cao (曹刘娟). He is also co-advised by Prof. Xuan Cheng (程轩) and Prof. Rongrong Ji (纪荣嵘). He received the M.S. degree from Guizhou University under the guidance of Prof. Yongjun Zhang (张永军), and the B.S. degree from Huaqiao University mentored by Prof. Hailin Li (李海林). His current research interest lies in 3D Vision.
